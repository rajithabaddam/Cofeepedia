Global Production Over Time


Streamgraphs are a type of stacked area chart where the areas are displaced around a central axis. They are often used for displaying compound volume across different categories or over time. These plots visualize both Arabica and Robusta coffee production by countries from 2001 to 2020, using a relative scale. Data values are reported in 1,000 60kg bags.

Brazil is consistently the largest producer of Arabica coffee, and Vietnam is consistently the largest producer of Robusta coffee. Since at least 2001, Vietnam has increased production.
