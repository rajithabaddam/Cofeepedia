Global Coffee Production & Consumption, 2019


This map illustrates the band just south of the equator representing The Coffee Belt AKA The Bean Belt, bounded by the Tropics of Cancer and Caporicorn. The wet, sunny climate and rich soil in this region are optimal growing conditions for coffee plants. Soil, climate and altitude all affect the flavor and aroma of the beans. Feel free to explore our flavor and aroma data on our Flavors page.

Source: National Coffee Association
